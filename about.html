Our Story

Thurayya Islam Media is a non-profit Islamic Media house based in Kampala Uganda whose primary goal is to avail Islamic knowledge to a vast pool of Muslims at zero cost.

Acquiring knowledge has been at the heart of the Islamic faith from the very beginning. This is elevated from the Holy Quran Surah 96:1-5 that prompted Muslims to read and acquire knowledge. Furthermore seen in the Prophetic Hadith “The Seeking of knowledge is obligatory to every Muslim male and female” (Narrated by Anas Ibn Malik, RadhiAllahu Anhu)

Here at Thurayya Islam Media we take pride in streaming Islamic audio and video lectures from renowned scholars, Islamic books for free download and so much more Islamic content.
